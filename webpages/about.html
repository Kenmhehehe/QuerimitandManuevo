About Maligno Pares Overload

Maligno Pares Overload stands as a beacon of culinary excellence, offering a tantalizing array of flavors and affordable dining options to discerning food enthusiasts. Our commitment to quality, value, and customer satisfaction sets us apart in the vibrant landscape of Filipino cuisine.

Our Offerings

At Maligno Pares Overload, we specialize in serving hearty bowls of pares, a delectable beef stew paired with unlimited rice, soup, and a refreshing beverage—all for the unbeatable price of Php 100. Our dedication to providing budget-friendly yet satisfying meals has made us a favorite among locals and visitors alike.
